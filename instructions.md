# Instructions

## Welcome to the Coral Data Toolkit

This application helps coral restoration practitioners collect and digitize field data efficiently.

---

## Workflow:

### 1. Plan Your Experiment
- Navigate to the "Experiment Planner" tab
- Enter your experiment name, start date, and starting temperature
- Input your coral sample IDs (one per line)
- Download the generated PDF layout sheet for data entry in the field

### 2. Conduct Your Experiment
- Use the layout on the sheet to organize your coral fragments on the color card
- Rows 1 & 3 are test fragments, rows 2 & 4 are control fragments

### 3. Digitize Your Data
- After collecting data, take a clear photo of your completed datasheet
- Navigate to the "Datasheet Upload" tab
- Upload the photo and enter the coral IDs in the same order
- The system will extract handwritten scores and export to CSV

---

## Tips for Best Results:

- Take photos in good lighting with minimal shadows
- Write clearly in the data entry boxes with a blue or black pen
- Ensure all 4 corner markers (black circles) are visible in photos
- Use the same layout every day of the experiment

---

## Data Entry Codes:

- **Digits 0-6**: VBS scores
- **Hyphen (-)**: Intentional "no data" (e.g., fragment died)
- **Empty box**: System reads as empty/missing
- **Low confidence (?)**: System will flag uncertain readings for review
